Rich Dad Poor Dad

Robert Kiyosaki

Rich Dad Poor Dad is a personal finance book written by Robert Kiyosaki. It explains the difference between the mindset of a “rich dad” and a “poor dad.” The book teaches that wealthy people focus on building assets, investing, and creating passive income, while others depend mainly on salaries. It encourages financial education, smart investing, and entrepreneurship. The main message is that understanding money and making it work for you is the key to achieving financial freedom and long-term wealth.
